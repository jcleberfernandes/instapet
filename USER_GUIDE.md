# InstaPet — User Guide

InstaPet is a social media platform for pet lovers. Share photos of your pets, follow other owners, and discover new animals through hashtags.

---

## Getting started

1. Open the app at **http://localhost** (or the address provided by your instructor).
2. Click **Register** and create an account with a username, email, and password.
3. Log in with your credentials — you will land on the feed.

---

## Main features

### Feed
The feed shows posts from everyone on the platform by default. Use the toggle at the top to switch between **All posts** and **Following only** (posts from people you follow). The **Explore** tab shows posts from accounts you do not yet follow.

### Creating a post
Click the **+** button in the navbar or the post form at the top of the feed. Add an image (JPEG, PNG, GIF or WebP — max 10 MB), write a caption, and include hashtags with the `#` symbol. Click **Post** to publish.

### Likes, saves, and comments
On any post card you can:
- **Like** — click the heart icon.
- **Save** — click the bookmark icon; saved posts are accessible from your profile.
- **Comment** — click the speech bubble to open the post detail and add a comment.

### Profiles
Click any username or avatar to view a profile. You will see the user's bio, their post grid, and counters for posts, followers, and following. Switch between the **Posts** and **Saved** tabs on your own profile.

### Following
Click **Follow** on any post card, search result, or profile page. The follower/following counters update instantly. Click the counter numbers on a profile to see the full list of followers or accounts they follow.

### Hashtags and search
Hashtags in captions are clickable links — click one to see all posts tagged with it. Use the **Search** page to find posts by hashtag directly. You can also follow users from the search results.

### Notifications
The bell icon in the navbar shows a badge when you have new notifications (likes, comments, follows). Click it to open the panel. Notifications are marked as read automatically when the panel opens, and you can clear them all at once.

### Editing your profile
Click your avatar or username in the navbar and select **Edit profile** to update your display name, bio, and avatar image.

### Editing and deleting posts
On your own posts, open the post detail (click the image) to find the **Edit** and **Delete** options.

---

## Tips

- Hashtags must start with `#` and contain no spaces (e.g. `#goldenretriever`).
- You can add multiple hashtags in one caption.
- The right sidebar on the feed shows the most popular tags — click one to filter posts.
- The **Terms & FAQ** page (link in the footer) answers common questions.
